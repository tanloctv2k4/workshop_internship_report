### Areas for Improvement

While I have achieved certain milestones, I recognize specific areas that need focused improvement to become more well-rounded:

* **Enhancing Discipline:** Commit to strictly and proactively complying with all workplace regulations, working hours, and standard operational processes of the organization.
* **Optimizing Problem-Solving Mindset:** Train myself to analyze technical incidents (especially complex system errors on the Cloud) systematically, identifying the root cause calmly to deliver permanent fixes rather than temporary workarounds.
* **Refining Communication and Collaboration:** Actively expand my daily and professional communication; learn to present technical problems concisely and clearly to collaborate smoothly and handle unexpected situations flexibly with team members.
